About Agricoza

Agricoza is a fully intergrated, user-friendly, farm management tool which aims to revolutionise the agriculture industry as we know it. We are targeting major problems in the south african agriculture space, such as, ridiculous food prices, low crop yield and ever-increasing expenses of running a farm.

many south african farms make use of little or no technology on a daily basis. farmers are still using paper-based records to keep track of important farm information and operations. Delegation of tasks are done manually on most farms, this is ineffective and unrealiable.

Our solutions to the afore-mentioned problems are as follows. A farm management system that that benefits all parties i.e farm owners, managers as well as the farm workers. We provide various records, reports and analytics to ease the burden of managing the farm. We also make work easier and more efficient by automating many manual processes across the farm.
