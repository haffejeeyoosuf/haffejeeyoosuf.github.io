Agricoza's Defining Features


User Friendly Interface

We noticed a common trend when looking at alternitives to our product, they all have a steep learning curve in order to use the software effectively.

This results in the system being abandoned due to complexity, thus reverting back to old ways.

Agricoza remedies this by providing a clean, sleek and beginner-friendly GUI to all our customers.

Automated Management Based On Weather Conditions and I.O.T Sensor Data

Agricoza's smart farm managment system uses sensor data togeather with weather forecasts to automate the delegation of tasks.

A task can automatically be assigned to an idle worker to water a field. E.g. if no rain is predicted and the water level on that field drops below the crops optimal level, a suggestion for assigning a task to fix the problem will be displayed.

View Pricing

Auto-Assign Tasks Based On Worker Availability

We have complex algorithms in place to ease the burden of farm management. Our system can detect idle workers or workers with the least amount of active tasks and assign new tasks respectively.

This ensures that no single worker is ever over worked.

Real-Time, Descriptive, and Predictive Analysis Reporting

We have the data, why not use it? Agricoza uses all of the required data to provide you with reliable, accurate, and up to date reports on just about anything useful.

We have historic data, real-time data, descriptive analysis and, predictive analysis for all your past present and future needs.
