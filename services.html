Agricoza's Defining Features


User Friendly Interface

We noticed a common trend when looking at alternatives to our product, they all have a steep learning curve in order to use the software effectively.

This results in the system being abandoned due to complexity, thus reverting back to old ways.

Agricoza remedies this by providing a clean, sleek and beginner-friendly GUI to all our customers.

View Pricing

Automated Farm Management Based On Weather Conditions and I.O.T Sensor Data

Agricoza's smart farm managment system uses sensor data together with weather forecasts to automate the delegation of tasks.

A task can automatically be assigned to an idle worker to water a field. E.g. if no rain is predicted and the water level on that field drops below the crops optimal level, a suggestion for assigning a task to fix the problem will be displayed.

View Pricing

Auto-Assign Tasks Based On Worker Availability

We have complex algorithms in place to ease the burden of farm management. Our system can detect idle workers or workers with the least amount of active tasks and assign new tasks respectively.

This ensures that no single worker is ever over worked.

View Pricing

Multi-Platform, Role Differentiation

We provide a very specific feature set based on the user's job.

Farm owners have the most features. They have all manager features, the ability to add managers, and also view detailed reports and analytics. Managers are provided with the necessary features required to assign tasks and run the farm daily. Lastly, farmworkers can receive notifications, call for help, and complete tasks.

Owners and managers will primarily use a PC, while workers will be limited to a mobile device. However, Owners and managers are provided with an android app that delivers limited but useful functionality to manage the farm on the go.

Historic Records, General and Predictive Reporting

Agricoza saves and stores all management activities securely for an in-depth history.

We have the data, why not use it? Agricoza uses all of the required data to provide you with reliable, accurate, and up to date reports on just about anything useful.

We have historic data, real-time data, descriptive analysis and, predictive analysis for all your past present and future needs.

View Pricing

Real-Time, Global Farm Chat

Our global farm chat allows for quick and easy communication between all the farm staff.

Farmworkers can easily request assistance on a particular field, they can also clarify any uncertainty by chatting to a manager, and lastly, the farm owner can get quick updates from the manager as well as give instructions without being too intrusive via a call.

This is a time-saving strategy and increases productivity within the workplace.
